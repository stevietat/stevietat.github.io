Cadee

Cadee is a social platform for golfers to track their progress and scores, discover new courses and places to play and compete against their friends.

Social feed with player and course updates.

Players compete against each other.

Detailed course pages allowed players to submit games they've played there, get updates, course maps and compete against other members.

Golfers track their progress over time.

Statistics on put accuracy.
