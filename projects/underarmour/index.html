Under Armour

Under Armour needs an app to help customers customize swag-bags at their many pop-up shops and sponsored events.

Customers are taken through a step-by-step process where they can pick what Under Armour items are going into their swag bag.

For each item customers can select the specific colour and size to ensure they get gear they can actually wear.

Product selection.

Customers select the exact product they need.

A handy navigation at the bottom allows customers to keep track of what outfit they're ultimately putting together.

Final swag-bags are shipped to customers later.
