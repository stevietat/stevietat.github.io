Athena

Athena allows trainers and coaches to easily follow up with their students, provide follow-up exercises and lessons, and easily keep track of their progress.

Individual student conversations and lessons are organized into streams that allow media, messages, quizzes and audio to be shared.

Mobile app contains all the desktop functionality.

Students can easily record audio assignments.

Students are easily managed at scale with groupings and full reports about their progress and who is lagging behind.

Coaches create lessons and courses with ease that students must progressively complete to finish units. All content is delivered to their students via mobile app.
